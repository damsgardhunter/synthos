1. Major Statistical Bias: Hydride Hypothesis Is Misleading

Your current hypothesis:

Hydrides priority: 0.9
count: 1
maxTc: 70
pipelinePasses: 0

This is a statistical artifact.

Your engine thinks hydrides are promising because they are under-sampled, not because they performed well.

Current situation:

Family	Count	Max Tc	Pipeline Passes
Carbides	29	153	3
Borides	41	124	2
Hydrides	1	70	0

The system should not assign 0.9 priority to a family with 1 data point.

Fix

Add confidence weighting:

familyConfidence = sqrt(count / totalSamples)
adjustedScore = avgScore * familyConfidence

or Bayesian prior:

adjustedScore = (sumScores + priorMean*k) / (count + k)

Example:

k = 10
priorMean = 0.5

This prevents single-sample bias.
