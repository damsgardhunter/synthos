Strong Correlation Physics Engine

Many high-Tc materials are strongly correlated systems.

Examples:

Material	Mechanism
Cuprates	Mott physics
Fe pnictides	spin fluctuations
Heavy fermions	Kondo lattice

Your engine includes Hubbard U/W but does not fully model correlated phases.

Tasks

Create module:

/physics/correlationEngine
Step 1 — Detect Correlated Regime

Condition:

U/W > 1

Signals:

Mott proximity
spin fluctuations
orbital ordering
Step 2 — Compute Correlation Score
correlationScore =
0.3 * U/W
+ 0.3 * DOS(EF)
+ 0.2 * flatBandScore
+ 0.2 * nesting
Step 3 — Adjust Pairing Weights

Modify pairing channels:

spinFluctuationWeight
orbitalFluctuationWeight
