2️⃣ Crystal Graph Neural Network (Structure-Aware Surrogate)

Your logs show predictions based on chemistry + heuristics:

dosEF
lambda
chargeTransfer

But modern systems use graph neural networks on the crystal structure.

Examples:

CGCNN

MEGNet

M3GNet

ALIGNN

MatterGen GNN

These learn from:

atomic species
bond distances
angles
coordination

Instead of hand-engineered descriptors.

Why this matters

Current surrogate models often fail when:

same chemistry
different structure

Example:

FeSe layered → superconducting
FeSe rocksalt → not superconducting

A GNN sees this difference.

GNN002: atomic embeddings
GNN003: message passing layers
GNN004: Tc prediction head
GNN005: training dataset integration

3️⃣ Generative Crystal Structure Model

Your system currently generates structures with:

heuristics
prototypes
diffusion attempts

The next upgrade is true generative crystal models.

Examples used in research:

DiffCSP

CDVAE

MatterGen

Crystal Diffusion Models

They generate:

composition
lattice
atomic positions

simultaneously.

Why this matters

Heuristic generators often produce:

impossible structures
overlaps
unstable bonding

Generative models learn real crystal statistics.

Example:

probability distributions of bond lengths
coordination numbers
space groups

Result:

valid structures increase 5×–20×

GEN003: generate lattice vectors
