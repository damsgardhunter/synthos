Core Concept

Instead of discovering equations like:

Tc = f(DOS, λ, phonon_freq)

the system discovers causal graphs:

phonon_freq → λ → Tc
pressure → phonon_freq
DOS → λ
topology → pairing symmetry

This identifies mechanisms, not just predictors.
