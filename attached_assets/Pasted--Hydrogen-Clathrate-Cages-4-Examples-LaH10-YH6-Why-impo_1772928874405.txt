. Hydrogen Clathrate Cages
4

Examples

LaH10

YH6

Why important

Hydrogen cages produce:

extremely high phonon frequencies

very strong electron-phonon coupling

Which drives very high Tc.

Generator rules

host = rare_earth | alkaline_earth
hydrogen_ratio = 4–10
structure = clathrate cage
pressure_bias = high

Design parameters

H-H distance

cage symmetry

metal charge transfer
