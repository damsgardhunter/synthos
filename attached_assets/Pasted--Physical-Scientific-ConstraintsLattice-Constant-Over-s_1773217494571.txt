 Physical & Scientific ConstraintsLattice Constant Over-simplification: The code defaults latticeConstant to 3.5 Å or simply picks the first element's constant found in the formula.Problem: This ignores the actual unit cell volume and stoichiometry. A material like $LaH_{10}$ at 200 GPa has a vastly different lattice constant than elemental $La$ at ambient pressure.Fix: Use a weighted average of atomic radii or a simple Murnaghan Equation of State fallback to scale the lattice constant based on the input pressure.Orbital Character Ambiguity: In computeWannierProjection, you determine the orbType (s, p, or d) by a simple "greater than" comparison of averages.Constraint: This fails to account for hybridization. A band might be $45\% d$ and $40\% p$. Labeling it purely as "d" is misleading for downstream models like the lambda-regressor.Improvement: Return a "Hybrid" tag if the top two characters are within $10\%$ of each other.Magic Number in Hoppings: The line bandwidth / (2 * Math.max(1, 6)) contains a hardcoded 6.Scientific Fix: This 6 likely represents a Cubic coordination number. This should be a variable derived from the latticeType (e.g., 4 for BCC, 12 for FCC) to make the "Effective Hopping" physically meaningful across different crystal systems.3.
